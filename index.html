What is Xhunt?

Xhunt is a small indie game that is a combination of an adventure game and a hangout game. Xhunt has maps and gamemodes that let the player do whatever they want. Meanwhile, on the adventure side, you can find out soon to be added lore and soon make your own path (darkmode map).
